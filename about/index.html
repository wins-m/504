About Me

I’m WM, a literate gorilla at large. For now (and temporarily), I work as a quantitative researcher specializing in China’s A-Share stock market. This means I won’t be sharing much related to that for the next few years.

I used to be something of a film buff - though that feels like ages ago. These days, I mostly watch films at home rather than visiting theaters, thanks to the lackluster cinephile culture (bad manners, honestly) in my city. Still, long live cinemas! One day, I hope to move to a real metropolis, one that suits the nature of a gorilla.

Welcome to my blog. In the Posts section, you’ll find all original content written by yours truly, with or without tags. The Notes section features select quotations from various anonymous sources, and the Collection section hosts articles from public sources (with the original URLs). If there’s any concern over rights, please feel free to contact me.
